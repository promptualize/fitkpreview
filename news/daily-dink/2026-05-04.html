FITK Daily Dink - May 4, 2026

Some of the top stories moving in pro pickleball today, with Chris Haworth shutting down 15-year-old Tama Shimabukuro for men's singles gold and Anna Leigh Waters and Ben Johns adding the mixed doubles title at Atlanta's Championship Sunday.

  • Haworth stops Shimabukuro's run in men's singles as Waters, Johns take mixed doubles title

    PPA TourVeolia Atlanta ChampionshipsChris HaworthTama ShimabukuroMen's Singles

    Pickleball.com recaps Carvana Championship Sunday at the Veolia Atlanta Pickleball Championships, where top seed Chris Haworth defeated 15-year-old Tama Shimabukuro 11-5, 11-1 to capture his seventh career PPA Tour men's singles title. Anna Leigh Waters and Ben Johns paired up to win the mixed doubles crown earlier in the same session, capping the opening finals at the last Slam before the PPA Finals in San Clemente.
