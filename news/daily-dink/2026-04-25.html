FITK Daily Dink - April 25, 2026

Some of the top stories moving in pro pickleball today, from a fresh All Florida Pro League Season 3 draft preview and PPA Tour storylines for the Veolia Atlanta Championships, to a new MLP partnership with Champions Series Pickleball and the freshly released MLP Austin match schedule.

  • All Florida Pro League previews Season 3 Draft livestream on YouTube

    All Florida Pro LeagueTour DevelopmentErik TiceFloridaDraft Preview

    The Dink published a Season 3 Draft preview for the All Florida Pro League, the regional pro pickleball circuit holding its draft Friday, April 24 at 7 p.m. ET on YouTube. Writer Erik Tice walks through the league's top available picks and team needs heading into the third campaign of Florida-based pro team play.

  • PPA Tour drops Veolia Atlanta Pickleball Championships storylines for April 27 to May 3

    PPA TourVeolia Atlanta ChampionshipsSchedulePPA Tour FinalsPeachtree Corners

    The PPA Tour released its full storyline package for the Veolia Atlanta Pickleball Championships, presented by Ensure Max Protein and running April 27 to May 3 at Life Time Peachtree Corners. The preview lays out the points distribution in Atlanta, the top contenders across all five pro divisions, and the final stakes for players still chasing a spot in the PPA Tour Finals.

    Read at PPA Tour →

  • Major League Pickleball partners with Champions Series Pickleball for senior pro events

    Major League PickleballChampions Series PickleballSenior PickleballPartnershipBusiness

    Major League Pickleball announced a new partnership with Champions Series Pickleball, the pro 50 plus league founded in 2022 as the National Pickleball League. Under the deal, Champions Series Pickleball events will be rebranded as MLP Champions Series, expanding MLP's footprint into pickleball's senior pro tier.

    Read at Pickleball.com →

  • Major League Pickleball releases MLP Austin match schedule for June 11 to 14

    Major League PickleballMLP AustinScheduleAustin Pickle Ranch2026 Season

    Major League Pickleball published the full match schedule for MLP Austin, running June 11 to 14 at Austin Pickle Ranch in Austin, Texas. The fourth event of the 2026 regular season slots between St. Louis and St. Petersburg on a calendar that opens with Dallas on May 22 and runs through New York later in the summer.
